Mental Health Portal

This project is a platform designed to raise awareness about student mental health and provide accessible support resources. As students face increasing stress, anxiety, and depression from academic and social pressures, the portal offers guidance, resources, and tools to help them manage their well-being.

Key features of the portal may include:

Resource Library: Articles, tips, and guides on mental health topics.
Counseling Access: Links or information to connect with counselors and support services.
Awareness & Education: Information to reduce stigma around seeking help.
User-Friendly Design: Simple, accessible interface to make students feel comfortable exploring mental health resources.

The goal of this project is to create a safe, supportive environment where students can learn about mental health, recognize signs of distress, and find help when needed.
