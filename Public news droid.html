Public News Droid

The Public News Droid project is an Android‑based news aggregation application developed to provide users with timely access to public news and local updates through an intuitive mobile interface. The project documentation and report were created using LaTeX on Overleaf, highlighting the structured formatting, professional layout, and clarity of presentation. Built using Android Studio with a backend database, the app allows users to register securely, authenticate via login, and browse categorized news content, including national, local, and topic-specific stories. Administrators play a key role in monitoring submissions to ensure the accuracy and relevance of content, while authenticated users can contribute news items to enhance community participation. The system’s design emphasizes responsiveness, ease of navigation, and real-time updates to deliver a reliable and efficient platform for accessing current events.
