Digital Logic Circuit

The project “Design and Analysis of Universality of NAND and NOR Gate, Synchronous Random Counters, 4-Bit Logic Units, and Universal Logic Gates” focuses on exploring the fundamental building blocks of digital logic circuits. It involves designing and analyzing the universality of NAND and NOR gates, which can be used to implement any logic function. The project also covers the design of synchronous random counters, 4-bit logic units, and universal logic gates to understand their operation, efficiency, and practical applications in digital systems. This work provides hands-on experience in circuit design, logic analysis, and digital system implementation.
